Frequently Asked Questions

Can I shave between laser sessions?

Yes, it is generally recommended to shave between laser hair removal sessions. However, other hair removal methods such as waxing or plucking should be avoided as they can interfere with the effectiveness of the treatment.

What are the pros and cons of laser hair removal?
  • Pros: Effective long-term reduction of hair growth, precision targeting, relatively quick treatment sessions.
  • Cons: Potential discomfort during treatment, multiple sessions required for optimal results, possibility of skin irritation or pigment changes.
What happens if I stop laser hair removal after 4 sessions?

If you stop laser hair removal after only 4 sessions, you may not achieve the desired long-term reduction in hair growth. Hair may gradually grow back, though potentially finer and lighter than before treatment.
