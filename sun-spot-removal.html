Frequently Asked Questions

Will hair grow back if I stop laser?

Yes, hair may grow back if you stop laser hair removal treatments. However, it may be lighter and finer than before treatment.

Is laser hair removal permanent?

Laser hair removal can lead to long-term hair reduction, but it is not always permanent. Some individuals may experience regrowth over time, though it is typically finer and lighter than before.

Why is my hair thicker after laser?

It is uncommon for hair to become thicker after laser hair removal. However, some individuals may experience temporary changes in hair texture or regrowth due to hormonal fluctuations or other factors.
